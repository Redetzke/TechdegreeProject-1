Hello, world! I’m Heather.

I live in gorgeously rainy (or sunny) Portland, Oregon.

Background

I earned a BFA with a focus in Painting & Public Art at PNCA. I worked for a number of years as a jewelry designer, specializing in upcycled materials, and later studied Landscape Architecture in the UofO Master's Degree Program. The MLA program was a continuation of my interest and training in designing engaging works of art for the public sphere which can help reflect or shape the local identity of communities. I've exhibited work with galleries around Portland and the PNW, and was curated into PICA's Time Based Art festival as half of the collaborative, Bottle Lightning Projects.
